# Fully Funded International Internships for Indian Students - 2026

Here is a comprehensive guide to **fully funded international research internships and programs** for Indian students looking to gain research experience abroad in 2026. I've organized these by region with all the key details you requested.

## üáØüáµ Japan

### NIG Summer Internship (National Institute of Genetics)

- **Eligibility**: Pre-final year UG/PG students in Life Sciences (3rd year of 4-year Bachelor's, 2nd year of 3-year Bachelor's, etc.); PhD students NOT eligible[^1][^2]
- **Duration**: 6 weeks (June 8 ‚Äì July 17, 2026)[^1]
- **Deadline**: **January 5, 2026**[^3][^1]
- **Funding**: Round-trip airfare + Accommodation[^1]
- **Documents**: CV, Motivation Letter (500 words), Academic Transcripts, 2 Recommendation Letters[^2][^4]
- **Link**: nig.ac.jp/jimu/soken/intern


### UTRIP (University of Tokyo Research Internship Program)

- **Eligibility**: Completed 1+ year of Bachelor's degree; enrolled in natural sciences; must be outside Japan[^5][^6]
- **Duration**: 6 weeks (June 25 ‚Äì August 5, 2026)[^6]
- **Deadline**: **January 7, 2026**[^7]
- **Funding**: Accommodation + Meals + Japanese Language Course[^6]
- **Documents**: CV, Personal Statement (700-1000 words), Transcripts, TOEFL/IELTS (if applicable), 1 Recommendation[^6]
- **Link**: s.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/utrip


### UTokyo Amgen Scholars Program

- **Eligibility**: Undergraduate students from Asian universities; completed 1+ year; not graduating before program[^8][^9]
- **Duration**: 9 weeks (June 4 ‚Äì August 6, 2026)[^9]
- **Deadline**: **February 1, 2026** (3 PM JST)[^10][^8]
- **Funding**: JPY 280,000 stipend (~‚Çπ1.62 lakh) + Travel + Accommodation[^8]
- **Documents**: CV, Statement of Purpose, Transcripts, 2 Recommendations via TAO portal[^9]
- **Link**: u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/prospective-students/amgen_program


### OIST Research Internship (Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology)

- **Eligibility**: Final 2 years of UG or Master's students[^11][^12]
- **Duration**: 3-6 months[^11]
- **Deadlines**: **October 15, 2025** (Spring 2026); **April 15, 2026** (Fall 2026)[^13][^11]
- **Funding**: JPY 2,400/day + Round-trip airfare + Accommodation + Visa support[^11]
- **Documents**: Statement of Purpose (400 words), CV, Transcripts[^11]
- **Link**: oist.jp/admissions/research-internship


### MEXT Research Students Scholarship

- **Eligibility**: Born after April 2, 1990; Bachelor's degree holders; good academic record[^14]
- **Duration**: 1.5-2 years (includes 6 months Japanese language training)[^14]
- **Deadline**: April-May 2025 (for 2026 admission)[^14]
- **Funding**: Full tuition + Living allowance + Airfare[^14]
- **Documents**: Application Form, Transcripts, Recommendation, Health Certificate, Research Plan[^15]
- **Link**: Embassy of Japan / studyinjapan.go.jp


### RIKEN IMS Internship

- **Eligibility**: Master's, PhD students, or young postdocs (within 3 years of PhD)[^16][^17]
- **Duration**: 1-3 months[^16]
- **Deadline**: 4 months before desired start date[^17]
- **Funding**: ¬•3,000/day + Airfare + Accommodation[^16]
- **Documents**: CV, Statement of Purpose, Research Background, GDPR Consent (EU/UK), Recommendation[^17]
- **Link**: ims.riken.jp/english/jobs/internship


### RIKEN CBS Summer Program

- **Eligibility**: Graduate students and postdoctoral researchers[^18]
- **Duration**: 2 months (June 9 ‚Äì August 7, 2026) for Plan A Internship[^18]
- **Documents**: CV, Research Background, Statement of Purpose
- **Link**: cbs.riken.jp/en/summer


### METI Japan Internship Program

- **Eligibility**: 18-40 years; Students/Graduates; JLPT N3 or English proficiency[^19][^20]
- **Duration**: 3-6 months[^20]
- **Funding**: ¬•3,000/day + Airfare + Accommodation + Mobile phone + Insurance[^20]
- **Documents**: CV, Enrollment/Graduation Proof, Language Certificate[^20]
- **Link**: internshipprogram.go.jp


### Kyoto University Amgen Scholars Program

- **Eligibility**: 1st-3rd year UG in science programs from Asian universities[^21][^22]
- **Duration**: 9 weeks (June 5 ‚Äì August 6, 2026)[^22]
- **Deadline**: **February 1, 2026** (3 PM JST)[^23]
- **Funding**: JPY 180,000 stipend + Travel + Accommodation[^21]
- **Link**: opir.kyoto-u.ac.jp/amgenscholars

- **Eligibility**: 2nd year Bachelor's or above; enrolled students from partner universities[^24][^25][^26]

- Many programs like **RISE Germany** are specifically for US/UK/Canada/Ireland students, so verify eligibility carefully before applying
